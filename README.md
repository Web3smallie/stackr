💡 What is Stackr?
Stackr is a three-in-one financial super app built on Solana and deeply integrated with Bags.fm:
💸 Stackr Pay
Get paid instantly by anyone, anywhere in the world. Create your personal payment page in 60 seconds, share your link, and start receiving SOL, USDC, USDT or Bags tokens immediately. No bank account. No KYC. No borders.
🔒 Stackr Vault
Save for what matters. Lock your crypto until your goal date — new phone, emergency fund, school fees, business capital. Like PiggyVest but built on Solana. Set your target, deposit, and watch your progress bar fill up.
📈 Stackr Pool
Invest together. Create or join community investment pools, vote on which Bags tokens to buy, and share the profits proportionally. Like a mutual fund but fully transparent, community governed, and onchain.
